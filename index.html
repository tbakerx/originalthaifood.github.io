Welcome !

We are a family run Thai food restaurant located in the heart of beautiful Ao Nang in Krabi, Thailand. You can find us centrally located along the main hotel and guest house street just a few minutes walk to the beach. We are open everyday of the week and serve up freshly caught seafood daily. We have a little something for everyone, from 50 baht pad thais for the budget-conscious to fine seafood dinners (at still affordable prices). Come and visit us today to see why so many first-time customers quickly become regulars!
